How we got started

STLF was founded in 2003 on the campus of the University of Minnesota and came to UW-Madison in 2009. The club gained traction through the Pay it Forward Tour that was multi-day volunteer trip focused on service, education, and reflection where students would travel to cities around the US to volunteer, explore, and participate in team bonding activities.
