General Info.

STLF volunteers in the city of Madison, and partners with clubs on UW-Madison's campus. Some of the organizations we volunteer with are Second Harvest Food Bank, Lakeshore Nature Preserve, Ronald McDonald House, and Box of Balloons.

On campus, we partner with the Red Cross to host blood drives as well as host campus clean up events and make tie blankets to give to the Womens shelter.

To join, you can go through the Wisconsin Involvement Network Website or can contact us through our email: pif.wisc@gmail.com to be added to our mailing list to hear more about upcoming volunteering events and meetings.
